### 谝闲传 [piàn hán chuán]
-----------

[Shaanxi dialect] n. small talk which not related with business / chat

谝[piǎn]: show off / chat

闲[xián]: leisure

传[chuán]: spread


e.g. 谝闲传 with some friends in weekend can make one relax.
