### 时尚 [shí shàng]
-----------
fashion

-----------

时 [shí] `time / when / period -> something related with time`

尚 [shàng] `to esteem / to praise highly because it's good`

e.g  `今天（jīn tiān）是时尚日（rì）` Today is fashion day.
