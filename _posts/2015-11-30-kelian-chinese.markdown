### 可怜的 [kě lián]
-----------

adj. pitiful; poor; pathetic

可[kě]:  may; can

怜[lián]: pity; sympathize

-----------

e.g. Poor(可怜的) Kevin got to airport at midnight and his car accidentally broke down, so he arrived at home in the morning.
