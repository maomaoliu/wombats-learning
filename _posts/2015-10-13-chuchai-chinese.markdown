### 出差 [chū chāi]
-----------

verb. to go on an official or business trip

出[chū]: o go out

差[chāi]: mission; to commission

-----------

e.g. Fei & Meng are in Melbourne. 她们([tā men] they)在([zài] are doing)出差.
