### 兄妹 xiōng mèi

-----------

the relation of elder brother and younger sister

兄 xiōng elder brother

弟 dì younger brother

姐 jiě elder sister

妹 mèi younger sister


`兄弟姐妹` brothers and sisters

Sample: `Fei and Trent are 兄妹`
