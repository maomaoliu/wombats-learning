### 元宵节 [yuán xiāo jié]
-----------

元宵: `sticky rice dumplings`

节: `festival`

灯笼: `lantern`


Example:

    在[zài]元宵节，我们都[dōu]会[huì]吃[chī]元宵、挂[guà]灯笼。

    In the Lantern Festival, all of us will eat sticky rice dumplings and hang lanterns.
