### 排队 [pái duì]
-----------

v. wait in line; queue up

排[pái]: arrange; put in order

队[duì]: a row of people; line

-----------

e.g. I waited in line for 2 hours to repair my laptop, but they took 10 minutes to check it and told me it works well.
