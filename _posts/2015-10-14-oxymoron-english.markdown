### noun [ˌɒksɪˈmɔːrɒn]
-----------

a figure of speech in which apparently contradictory terms appear in conjunction.
when two contradictory terms are used together.

"gun safety is an oxymoron"

Other examples:
Clearly confused
Act naturally
Deafening silence
Pretty ugly
Virtual reality
Run slowly
