### 不明觉厉 [bù míng jué lì]
-----------

不，no

明，understand

觉，feel

厉，awesome，wonderful 

不明觉厉，Admiration arises despite my incomprehension of what you said. 

e.g. I feel '不明觉厉' after hearing your session.
