### 开心 kāi xīn {% include play.html param="kaixin-main" %}

-----------
feel happy / have a great time.       

我很开心 {% include play.html param="kaixin-sample-1" %}
-----------
I'm very happy.

我不开心 {% include play.html param="kaixin-sample-2" %}
---------
I don't feel happy.
	
    开(kāi)：open
    心(xīn)：heart / mind
    很(hěn)：very much
    不(bù)：not
