### 水货 [shuǐ huò]
-----------

smuggled goods

-----------

水　`water`

货　`goods`

水货手机 `smuggled phones`

At the beginning, the smuggler in China transport goods which are highly taxed to the domestic by sea.
So called like `goods on water`.
