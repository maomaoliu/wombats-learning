### 叶公好龙 [yè gōng hào lóng]
-----------

  pretend to like something which one really fears

-----------

叶公　`Lord Ye`

好　  `like`

龙    `dragon`

#### e.g.
------
Xiaojun like scala, that is 叶公好龙.
