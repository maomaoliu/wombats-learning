### 忐忑 [tǎn tè]
-----------

  perturbed

-----------

   忐：composed of 上(shàng) and 心(xīn)
   
   忑：composed of 下(xià) and 心(xīn)

-----------

   上: up
   
   下: down
   
   心: heart

-----------

e.g
     Kevin feels very 忐忑 when deploying Jetwire.
