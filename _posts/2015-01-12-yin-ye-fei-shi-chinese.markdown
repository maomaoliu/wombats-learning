### 因噎废食 [yin1 ye4 fei4 shi2]

-----------
idiom

- stop eating for fear of choking

- refuse to do what one should for fear of running a risk

因 because

噎 choke

废 stop

食 eat


#### Example sentences
------------
Though there're risks for this solution, but we shouldn't 因噎废食.
