### nom nom [nɒmˌnɒm]
-----------
exclamation \nɒmˌnɒm\

- used to express pleasure at eating, or at the prospect of eating, delicious food.

`Xi'an style cumin lamb ribs for dinner, nom nom!`

- noun. delicious food.

`there were all kinds of nom noms—onion rings, hot dogs, burgers, and fries`
