### 好(hǎo)

ok/good 
---------

好的(hǎo de)
ok

好吗?(hǎo ma)
ok?

好啊!(hǎo a)
ok!

好吧...(hǎo ba)
ok...

好了(hǎo le)
now things are ok

好啦!(hǎo lā)
ok,that's enough!
