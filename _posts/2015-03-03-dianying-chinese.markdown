### 电[diàn] 影[yǐng]
-----------
noun.

电: `electronic`

影: `image/picture/reflection/shadow`

### Pattern:

    电X: describe something powered by electronic

      电 + 话(speak) = telephone
      电 + 灯(light) = light
      电 + 视(vision) = television
      电 + 梯(ladder) = lift/elevator
      ...


Example:

    - 我们去看电影吧

    - let's go to watch a movie
