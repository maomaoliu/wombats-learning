### 羊 yáng

-----------

goat, sheep

山羊(shān yáng): goat
                 
绵羊(mián yáng): sheep

2015 is the year of the goat.

生肖(shēng xiào): the Chinese Zodiac

The bronzes(sculptures) were once part of a fountain that displayed the 12 animals of the chinese zodiac at the old summer palace.
