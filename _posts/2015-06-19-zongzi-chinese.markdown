### 粽子　zòng　zǐ
-----------

  a pyramid-shaped dumpling made of glutinous rice wrapped in bamboo or reed leaves (eaten during the dragon boat festival)

-----------
　on Dragon Boat Festival, all Chinese eat Zong Zi.
