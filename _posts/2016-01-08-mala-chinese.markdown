### 麻辣 [má là]
-----------

n. hot and numbing taste

麻[má]: numbing; can't feel your mouth

辣[là]: peppery; hot

-----------

e.g. 麻辣鸡 （hot and numbing taste chicken）麻辣香锅（hot and numbing taste pot）
