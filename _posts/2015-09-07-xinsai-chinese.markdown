### 心塞 [xīn sāi]
-----------

  A popular adjective word from internet
  
  Describes a status or sad/sorrow feeling like your heart is plugged.
  
  Can't breath

-----------

   心 `heart`

   塞 `plug`


e.g

     心塞啊！
