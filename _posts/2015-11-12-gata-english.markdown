### noun [gata] (Spanish)
-----------

Gata: This is a spanish word for (Female) "cat". Male cat is gato

"esos ojos de gata"

esos: those

ojos: eyes

of: of

gata: cat (female)


"esos ojos de gata" - those (female) cat eyes
